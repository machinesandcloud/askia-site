Offer acceptance should feel clear, not rushed.

If you are still uncertain about package, scope, or manager quality, the work is not done yet.

## What to confirm before you say yes

Check:

- base, equity, bonus, and sign-on
- title and level
- reporting line
- scope and expectations
- start date
- remote or hybrid details
- review timing and growth path

If something matters, get it clarified before you accept.

## Why this matters

Candidates sometimes negotiate well and still make a weak final decision because they do not slow down for the last check.

That is avoidable.

## Final takeaway

Acceptance is not the moment to switch off your judgment.

It is the moment to confirm that the role you are saying yes to is actually the one you think you are getting.
